YOUR ART COULD BE FEATURED ON A BILLBOARD IN NYC We are looking for MSCHF Billboard designs to post on digital billboards all around the country. Winners will receive: - 1 month Discord Nitro - 1 month Funimation - "Godlike Artist" title in this server - Art featured on a billboard in NYC, and possibly elsewhere Submit your designs in the #billboard-competition channel. - To vote for an entry you like, click the react under the entry. - Be aware that you are restricted to one image or video submission every 30 minutes, but you are free to discuss your work elsewhere. - Channel mods and judges are @Jarrett, @Purge-Worthy Mod, and @Jian-Yang (Billboard King) General guidelines and rules: - Don't incorporate a QR code into your design, your submission will be disregarded. - One example of a good billboard size is 752 x 208 pixels (aspect ratio of 47:13) Winners will be chosen on Feb. 28 2020 at noon EST Credit for this description goes to @Jian-Yang (Billboard King) (edited)
